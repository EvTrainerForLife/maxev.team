Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

MaxEV ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MaxEV mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

MaxEV does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App functions entirely on your device. No account creation is required.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. For information on how Apple handles your data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App — including party names and EV training progress — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

MaxEV does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted at maxev.team/privacy.
